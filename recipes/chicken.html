Only Chicken is Real

Description

It s a chicken recipe. We like it in Bangladesh.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Chilies
  • Chicken
  • Chicken masala

Steps

  1. Wash chicken
  2. Mix all the masala with chicken
  3. Cook for 30 mins
  4. Add hot water and salt
  5. Cook for 20 mins again in low temperature
